Data-efficient Vision Transformers

IEEE IJCNN 2025, Rome, Italy

About the Special Session

Vision Transformers (ViTs) have emerged as a pivotal innovation in computer vision, achieving exceptional performance. This special session focuses on addressing challenges related to data scarcity, exploring innovative techniques like self-supervised learning, transfer learning, and model optimisation.
